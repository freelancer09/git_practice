This is the beginning of my exciting story.
By Michael McLaughlin

I was born to a small flock of colorful hummingbirds.

(P1) I had to eat rocks when I was little.  My Mom said 
they were meatballs but I knew they were rocks.

(P2) I was born to a happy waddle of penguins

(P3) I was born into a murder of crows.

(PN) I was born into a gaggle of geese.

(P3) My favorite food is bacon.
